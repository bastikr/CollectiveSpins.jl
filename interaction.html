Dipole-Dipole interaction¶

Of course the core of this library are the equations describing the dipole-dipole interaction and the collective decay

\[\begin{split}\Gamma_{ij} &= \frac{3}{2} \Gamma F_{ij}(k_a r_{ij}) \\ \delta \omega_{ij} &= \frac{3}{4} \Gamma G_{ij}(k_a r_{ij})\end{split}\]

with

\[\begin{split}F_{ij}(\xi) &= \big( 1 - (\vec{e}^{(r)} . \vec{e}^{(d_{eg})})^2 \big) \frac{\sin \xi}{\xi} + \big( 1 - 3 (\vec{e}^{(r)} . \vec{e}^{(d_{eg})})^2 \big) \big( \frac{\cos \xi}{\xi^2} - \frac{\sin \xi}{\xi^3}\big), \\ G_{ij}(\xi) &= - \big(1 - (\vec{e}^{(r)} . \vec{e}^{(d_{eg})})^2 \big) \frac{\cos \xi}{\xi} + \big( 1 - 3 (\vec{e}^{(r)} . \vec{e}^{(d_{eg})})^2 \big) \big( \frac{\sin \xi}{\xi^2} - \frac{\cos \xi}{\xi^3}\big).\end{split}\]

They are implemented in the functions:

  • interaction.Omega(a, θ, γ)
  • interaction.Gamma(a, θ, γ)

To create the interaction matrices the following two shortcuts are provided:

  • interaction.GammaMatrix
  • interaction.OmegaMatrix
